I am a Machine Learning PhD student at Georgia Tech advised by Zsolt Kira and Dhruv Batra. I am interested in embodied AI and reinforcement learning. From Summer 2022 to Winter of 2022, I interned at Meta AI working with Akshara Rai and Ruta Desai. From Summer to Winter of 2021 I interned at Meta AI working with Franziska Meier on inverse reinforcement learning. I completed my undergrad in Computer Science at the University of Southern California with a minor in Mathematics, where I worked with Joseph Lim. My email is "andrewszot 1 @ gmail com" (no spaces).
